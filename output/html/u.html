1 verb If you used to do something, you did it regularly or for a period of time in the past, but you don't do it now. I used to catch the ferry to work, but now I take the train. I never used to like coffee, but now I've got quite a taste for it. How often did you use to visit him?

2 adjective If you are used to something, you are familiar with it. It isn't a surprise or a shock to you. Don't take any notice of Tren's tantrumsyou'll get used to them. It took me quite a while to get used to the climate. I'm not used to having to wear a suit to work.

3 adjective If something such as a car is described as used, it is second-hand.
